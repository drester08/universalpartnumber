Definition

The number belongs to the part

A UPN identifies an item of supply: a physical thing defined by its specification.

It does not change when the manufacturer, supplier, catalogue, or price changes.

One item of supply. One permanent number.

The problem

One part. Different catalogues.

These codes describe the same cable ladder, but neither catalogue can resolve the other.

O-Line PS75
same item
HCL HCLZ 1010 075 100 4

A UPN gives the shared item one identifier. Manufacturer part numbers remain searchable aliases.

Issuance

How a UPN is issued

The registry issues the number from the specification. Nobody types or chooses it.

  1. 01

    Describe the part

    Category, dimensions, material, rating, and other defining attributes.

  2. 02

    Normalise and match

    Equivalent terms are mapped to one vocabulary and checked against existing records.

  3. 03

    Return the number

    A match returns its existing UPN. A new specification receives a new one.

1/2" · 0.5" · DN15 · 15NB→one size
S316L · 316L · SS316L · 1.4404→one material
Class 150 · 150# · PN20→one pressure rating

Format

Short, permanent, field-readable

The identifier carries no specification. The record beside it can be corrected and versioned without changing the number.

identifiercheck digit
  • Unambiguous alphabetO/0, I/1, S/5, and B/8 are excluded.
  • Check digitA transcription error is rejected before the number is accepted.
  • Readable aloudShort enough for a radio call in a workshop, plant, or pit.

Trust

Identity is not approval

A UPN identifies the item of supply. Whether it is suitable for a particular application remains an engineering decision.

TierEvidenceAutomatic action
Manufacturer-verifiedThe manufacturer confirmed the mappingPermitted
Engineer-signedA named, qualified engineer signed the mappingPermitted
Registry-inferredThe recorded specifications matchNot permitted

Every mapping records who made it, when, and from which source. Corrections create a new version; they do not erase the record.
